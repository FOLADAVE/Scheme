HEALTHY LIVING

“The greatest wealth is health.”

A healthy lifestyle is important for everyone. When we look after our physical health, we feel better too – fitter, more relaxed and better able to cope with things. Physical activity and exercise is a major contributor to a healthy lifestyle; people are made to use their bodies, and disuse leads to unhealthy living. ‘Healthy living’ means maintaining a healthy lifestyle and introducing habits that improve your health.

Paying attention to what you eat, being physically active, and learning more about your food and yourself can help you meet your health goals. Making changes to improve your health can lead to benefits for your body, your mind, your wallet, and even the environment.

Eating right and sleeping right will also help in maintaing a healthy lifestyle. Exercising the body keeps the body fit, it aids in weigth management and improves your quality of sleep. Regular excercise improves your body strength and boost your endurance.

Drink water regularly to stay healthy, it prevents dehydration. Research shows that headache is one of the most common sign of dehydration. What’s more, some studies have shown that drinking water can help relieve headaches in those who experience frequent headaches.

SUMMARY

Drinking water may help reduce headaches and headache symptoms. However, more high quality research is needed to confirm this potential benefit.

BENEFITS OF HEALTHY LIVING

A healthy lifestyle benefits you in the follow ways:

  • Decreased risk of disease : The more time a person doesn't smoke, eats healthy, exercises regularly, maintains healthy blood pressure, blood sugar and cholesterol levels and maintains a normal weight, the less likely they are to develop diseases such as hypertension, diabetes, chronic kidney disease and cardiovascular disease.

  • Increased happiness and less depression: Making healthy choices about your diet can make you feel emotionally stronger. You're doing something positive for yourself, which lifts your self-esteem. A good diet helps your brain and body work efficiently, too. Aim to have a balanced diet that includes all the main food groups.

  • Saves money:A healthy lifestyle leads to less hospital visit, less buying of drugs for treatment of ailments which in turn also saves your money.

  • Taking full control of your life: Healthy living gives you full control of your live.

TIPS ON POSITIVE HEALTH HABITS

The keys to keeping a healthy lifestyle are:
  • START SLOWLY - making a change gradually can be easier than doing all at once.
  • MAKE CHANGES A STEP AT A TIME - take note of the benefit that occur from balancing your meals or getting enough sleep time before jumping into another change.
  • TRY EXTENDING WHAT YOU ENJOY DOING - for instance, if you enjoying taking walks, through extending the routes you walk through by a managable amount.
